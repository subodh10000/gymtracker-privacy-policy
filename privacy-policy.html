Privacy Policy for GymTrackerApp

Last updated: December 2025

GymTrackerApp (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Fitness-related preferences and goals
  • Workout configuration and exercise completion status
  • Reminder preferences (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized workout plans
  • Track exercise completion during workouts
  • Display progress indicators within the App
  • Support optional reminders if you enable notifications

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple technologies such as UserDefaults and SwiftData. GymTrackerApp does not store personal data on external servers or cloud databases as part of normal app use.

4. Backend Processing (Workout Plan Generation)

During the initial workout plan generation process, certain profile or preference information may be temporarily transmitted to a secure backend service to generate your personalized workout plan. This data is processed only for this purpose and is not stored on the server after processing is complete.

5. Data Sharing

GymTrackerApp does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

6. User Control and Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the App using the “Delete Account” option available in the Profile section. When confirmed, this action permanently deletes all locally stored data associated with your account and resets the App to its initial state.

You may also remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the App from your device.

7. Notifications

GymTrackerApp may request permission to send optional local notifications to support your fitness routine. Notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device and do not require server communication.

  • Workout Reminders: Encourage daily workout completion
  • Hydration Reminders: Encourage regular hydration

Notification Control:

  • Notification permission is optional and requested during onboarding
  • You can enable or disable notifications at any time via device settings
  • The App functions normally even if notifications are disabled
  • No personal data is transmitted externally to deliver notifications

8. Children’s Privacy

GymTrackerApp is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be temporarily transmitted to and processed in the United States (US Central region) solely to generate your personalized workout plan during initial setup. Your data is not stored on the server after processing.
