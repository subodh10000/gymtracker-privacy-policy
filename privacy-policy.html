Privacy Policy for GymTrackerApp

Last updated: July 2025

GymTrackerApp ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

GymTrackerApp may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Fitness-related preferences and goals
  • Workout configuration and exercise completion status
  • Reminder preferences (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized workout plans
  • Track exercise completion during workouts
  • Display progress indicators within the App
  • Support optional reminders if you enable notifications

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple technologies (such as UserDefaults and SwiftData). GymTrackerApp does not store your personal data on external servers or cloud databases as part of normal app use.

4. Backend Processing (Workout Plan Generation)

During the initial workout plan generation process, certain profile or preference information may be temporarily transmitted to a secure backend service to generate your personalized workout plan. The data is processed only for this purpose and is not stored on the server after processing is complete.

5. Data Sharing

GymTrackerApp does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

6. User Control and Data Deletion

You may delete your data at any time by resetting the App's data (if available) or by uninstalling the App, which removes all locally stored data from your device.

7. Notifications

GymTrackerApp may request permission to send you optional local notifications to support your fitness routine. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device and do not require server communication.

  • Workout Reminders: Daily reminders to encourage you to complete your planned workout for the day. Reminder messages may reference the general workout focus for the day.
  • Hydration Reminders: Daily reminders to encourage regular hydration as part of a healthy routine.

Notification Control:

  • Notification permission is requested during onboarding and is entirely optional.
  • You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device's settings (Settings > GymTrackerApp > Notifications).
  • If notifications are disabled, the App will continue to function normally.
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers to deliver notifications.

8. Children's Privacy

GymTrackerApp is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be temporarily transmitted to and processed in the United States (US Central region) to generate your personalized workout plan. This occurs only during the initial setup process. Your data is not stored on the server after the workout plan is generated. By using the App, you consent to this temporary transfer for the purpose of generating your workout plan.
