Privacy Policy for GymTrackerApp

Last updated: March 2025

GymTrackerApp (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

GymTrackerApp may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Gender
  • Height and weight
  • Fitness goals and preferences
  • Workout activity, progress, and reminders

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized workout plans
  • Track workout progress and statistics
  • Display fitness analytics
  • Provide reminders and core app functionality

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using Apple technologies such as UserDefaults and SwiftData.

GymTrackerApp does not store your personal data on external servers or cloud databases.

4. Backend Processing

During the initial workout plan generation process, certain profile information may be temporarily transmitted to a secure backend service to generate your personalized workout plan. This data is processed only for this purpose and is not stored on the server after processing is complete.

5. Notifications

If you enable notifications, the App may send you reminders related to workouts or app activity. Notification preferences can be managed at any time through your device settings.

6. Data Sharing

GymTrackerApp does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

7. User Control and Data Deletion

You may delete all your data at any time by resetting the App or uninstalling it from your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

GymTrackerApp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your profile information may be temporarily transmitted to and processed in the United States (US Central region) to generate your personalized workout plan. This occurs only during the initial setup process. Your data is not stored on our servers after the workout plan is generated. By using the App, you consent to this temporary transfer for the purpose of generating your workout plan.
