Privacy Policy for GymTrackerApp

Last updated: July 2025

GymTrackerApp (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

GymTrackerApp may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Fitness-related preferences and goals
  • Workout configuration and exercise completion status
  • Reminder preferences (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Generate personalized workout plans
  • Track exercise completion during workouts
  • Display progress indicators within the App
  • Support optional workout and hydration reminders

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple technologies such as UserDefaults and SwiftData. GymTrackerApp does not store personal data on external servers or cloud databases during normal app use.

4. Backend Processing (Workout Plan Generation)

During initial workout plan generation, limited profile or preference information may be temporarily transmitted to a secure backend service solely to generate your personalized plan.

This data is processed only for that purpose and is not stored on the server after processing is complete.

5. Data Sharing

GymTrackerApp does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

6. User Control & Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the App by using the "Delete Account" option available in the Profile section.

When confirmed, this action permanently deletes all locally stored data associated with your account, including profile information, workouts, records, and reminders, and resets the App to its initial state.

You may also remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the App from your device.

7. Notifications

GymTrackerApp may request permission to send optional local notifications to support your fitness routine. Notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device and do not require server communication.

  • Workout Reminders: Daily reminders to encourage completing your planned workout. Messages may reference the general workout focus for the day.
  • Hydration Reminders: Daily reminders encouraging healthy hydration habits.
Notification Control:
  • Notification permission is optional and requested during onboarding
  • You can enable or disable notifications at any time in device settings
  • The App functions normally even if notifications are disabled
  • No personal data is sent to external servers to deliver notifications

8. Children’s Privacy

GymTrackerApp is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. International Users

If you use the App from outside the United States, limited information may be temporarily transmitted to and processed in the United States (US Central region) solely for workout plan generation during setup. No data is retained on the server after processing.
